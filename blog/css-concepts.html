Margins Vs Border VS Padding

What Are Marigns?

Margins are the transparent areas around the borders of content. Margins are used to position items on a webpage.

What Are Borders?

Borders are the areas that surround content and its padding. Borders can be made transparent, can have their color changed, or even be solid, or dotted.

What is Padding?

Padding clears an area around the content, and creates space between the content and the border. Think of padding as the background of the content.
